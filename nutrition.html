Nutrition Action Plan

Eating a diet rich in fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and omega-3 fatty acids supports brain health. Follow these tips:

  • Include foods high in antioxidants, such as berries and leafy greens.
  • Include omega-3-rich foods in your diet, such as flaxseeds, chia seeds, or algae-based supplements, at least once a week
  • Stay hydrated and limit processed foods.
